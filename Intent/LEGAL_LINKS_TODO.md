## Before publishing updates

1. Replace `[LEGAL_ENTITY_NAME]` and `[EFFECTIVE_DATE]` in `Intent/privacy.md` and `Intent/terms.md` when confirmed.
2. Confirm cross-links between documents resolve correctly.
3. Verify each URL opens in a browser without login.
4. Confirm Privacy Policy and Terms match current app behavior (storage, subscriptions, notifications, no analytics or ads). The Support page is intentionally minimal.
