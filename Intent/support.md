## Common topics

### Access and billing

Intent is currently offered at no charge. No subscription purchase is required in this release. Subscription UI is disabled, and StoreKit subscriptions are not offered to users.

If subscription features are introduced in a future version, subscriptions, trials, cancellations, and refunds would be managed through Apple ID → Subscriptions.

### Privacy

Intent stores app data on your device and may sync supported data through your private iCloud account when iCloud is enabled. The app does not use user accounts.

Read the Privacy Policy: https://github.com/atm-pet-projects/LegalMain/blob/main/Intent/privacy.md
